WELCOME TO YAMAGUCHI
KARATE ACADEMY.
Established in 1983, Yamaguchi Karate Academy in Hyderabad is led by Grandmaster R.K. Krishna Sir, who has over 40+ years of experience. Our academy has produced more than 6000 black belt champions.
Grandmaster Krishna Sir is an expert in Traditional Karate, Sports Karate, Yogic Karate, Self Defence, Chakra Balancing, Weight Management, and curing long-term diseases. He has won many awards and is highly respected in the martial arts world.
At Yamaguchi Karate Academy, we aim to improve both your body and mind. Our programs include self-defense, fitness, and techniques for inner peace and balance. Whether you want to get
stronger, healthier, or more focused, we have the right training for you. Join us and become part of our successful and supportive community. Experience the best in martial arts education with us.
